About UK Benefits Calculator

UKBenefitsCalculator.co.uk is a free, independent tool for UK residents. It helps you estimate your eligibility and entitlement for key state benefits — Universal Credit, Housing Benefit, Council Tax Reduction, Pension Credit and Child Benefit.

What this site is for

Use the benefits calculators to get quick eligibility estimates for the most common UK state support. The site is designed for households checking what they may be entitled to after a change in income, employment status or living situation. For formal claims and official entitlement decisions, use GOV.UK or contact the DWP directly.

The guides explain benefit rules in plain English — income thresholds, taper rates, how savings affect awards, how benefits interact. For formal advice, consult Citizens Advice or a benefits specialist.

Editorial approach

Every guide and calculator is reviewed against current DWP, GOV.UK and HMRC guidance. We prioritise factual accuracy and practical usefulness. Where assumptions are made, they are documented on the methodology page.

Our editorial standards cover how we write, review and update content. This site does not give legal or financial advice.

How the site is maintained

  • Benefit rates, thresholds and taper rates are updated each April when DWP announces uprating figures.
  • Guides are reviewed at each relevant policy change — including Budget announcements that affect Universal Credit, Housing Benefit or tax credits.
  • Pages are updated when users report unclear assumptions or outdated figures.
  • We do not publish pages without practical value for a UK benefits audience.

Editorial ownership and contact

Editorial owner: James Whitfield, a UK personal finance writer focused on state benefits, welfare policy and household income. James writes about Universal Credit, Pension Credit and the wider UK benefits system against DWP and GOV.UK guidance.
