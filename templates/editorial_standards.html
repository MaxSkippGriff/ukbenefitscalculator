Editorial Standards

AfterTaxSalary publishes calculator pages, salary pages and guides for UK salary planning. Our editorial standard is practical accuracy: pages should help a user make a better decision about take-home pay, not just restate tax definitions.

  • We prioritize factual accuracy and plain-English clarity.
  • Rates and thresholds are based on official public sources.
  • Guides are reviewed and updated when tax-year assumptions change.
  • We avoid unsupported claims and do not provide personal financial advice.
  • Every page should be useful without requiring JavaScript for core text.

How we write and review content

  • Pages are written in plain British English and checked for practical readability.
  • Calculator assumptions and guide explanations are reviewed together so content matches current tool logic.
  • We prefer worked examples, comparison links and actionable explanations over generic tax definitions.
  • We update titles, metadata and internal links when a page’s purpose changes (for example, new tax-year assumptions or new comparison tools).

Corrections and feedback

If you spot a mistake in a guide or think a calculator assumption is unclear, contact us via the contact page. We review correction requests and update pages where appropriate. For calculation-method details, see the methodology page.
