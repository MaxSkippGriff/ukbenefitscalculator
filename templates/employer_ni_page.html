Employment Allowance impact

Employment Allowance allows eligible employers to reduce their annual employer NI bill by up to £10,500. If fully available, NI due on this salary after the allowance offset is {{ with_allowance.ni_due|money }}.

Eligibility note: sole-director companies may be excluded. Confirm eligibility with HMRC or your accountant. See the Employment Allowance guide for details.

Under-21 and apprentice relief

Under-21 employees and apprentices under 25 attract 0% employer NI up to the upper secondary threshold (£{{ "{:,}".format(ni_current.upper_secondary_threshold|int) }}), then 15% above that. Use the full calculator to model relief scenarios.
